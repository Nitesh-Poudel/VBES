Message From The Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Educators,


Welcome to Vidya Bikash English Secondary School, a center for quality education in Gothatar. Our school is a vibrant learning community where innovation and tradition blend seamlessly to provide an enriching educational experience from Nursery to Grade 12. “Education for Excellence” is our motto. It is a commitment to nurturing excellence in every aspect of our students’ education. We strive to unlock the full potential of each student, guiding them towards academic success, personal growth, and the ability to excel in their future endeavors. We are on a continuous journey of improvement, learning from our experiences and integrating new innovations in the field of education. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our student-focused activities and regular teacher development programs that foster a nurturing and positive learning environment. Our approach to teaching is characterized by kindness and a positive disciplining approach, ensuring that each student feels valued and empowered. We believe in the holistic development of our students, preparing them not just academically, but also as compassionate and responsible global citizens. As we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of education, our school remains committed to providing a learning environment that prepares our students for the complexities of the modern world. Our objectives are multifaceted, aiming not only to impart knowledge but also to foster essential life skills that will serve our students throughout their lives. We believe in nurturing critical thinking and analytical competencies, enabling our students to make informed decisions and approach problems with innovative solutions. Our curriculum is designed to encourage an engaging learning process, utilizing technology and innovative teaching methods to make education interactive and enjoyable. We are aware that the consequences of low investment in children can be detrimental. In conclusion, our school is dedicated to the principle that investing in children is investing in our future. We are committed to providing an education that not only imparts knowledge but also builds character, fosters innovation, and prepares our students for the challenges and opportunities ahead. Join us as we strive to shape the future, one student at a time.

Warm regards,
Shree Ram Dahal
