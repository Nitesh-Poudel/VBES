Welcome to Vidya Bikash English School

Our school is guided by our Honorable principal Mr. Shree Ram Dahal, an esteemed academician, the current president of both FECAN and PABSON, Kageshwori Manohara, the institution truly lives up to its motto. “Education for Excellence”. This principle shows the school’s steadfast dedication to providing top-notch education that fosters both academic excellence and overall student growth which helps the student to be mental and physically strong.
