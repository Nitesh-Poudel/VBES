Welcome to Vidya Bikash English School

Our school is located in Kageshwori Manohara-8, Kathmandu with motive of providing the quality education to students range from play group to grade 12. The school has positioned itself as a central part of the community, committed to promoting high academic achievement and overall growth in its students. Our school is guided by our Honorable principal Mr. Shree Ram Dahal, an esteemed academician, the current president of both FECAN and PABSON, Kageshwori Manohara, the institution truly lives up to its motto. “Education for Excellence”. This principle shows the school’s steadfast dedication to providing top-notch education that fosters both academic excellence and overall student growth which helps the student to be mental and physically strong.
