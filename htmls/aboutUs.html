About Us

Welcome to

Vidya Bikash English School

Vidya Bikash English School provides a carefully planned curriculum that teaches students knowledge and important life skills, getting them ready for a successful future. The school emphasizes arts, sports, and literature with specific programs in music, dance, basketball, football, and table tennis. The school has shown great discipline and coordination by securing the March past champion title in both the Rastrapati Running Shield Competition and the FECA interschool March Past Competitions for the second year in a row. Outstanding student accomplishments showcase the school’s encouraging atmosphere, such as Miss Asna Shresha’s three bronze medals in badminton at the South Asia Regional Junior Badminton Competition, and Master Arjan Dahal’s several accolades including the Bagmati Singing Idol title. The school has highly qualified teachers, ongoing teacher training, and modern facilities like large play areas, a conference room, a fully-equipped library, and an up-to-date science laboratory, alongside a cafeteria serving healthy food options. Vidya Bikash’s educational philosophy focuses on kindness, positive discipline, and creative teaching techniques, positioning it as a key component of comprehensive education in Kathmandu.

Our school really pays the strong attention to students build up in both mental and physical way. Along with studying it is more important to include or make students more active in both overall inside and outside activities which will help them to increase their creativity, skills, brain capacity and also mental stability. That’s why we provide various platform and opportunities to the students to show their skills and talents. Our students’ involvement in different interschool competitions shows the school’s commitment to promoting a competitive mindset and collaboration. The many awards earned by the students showcase their extraordinary abilities and the school’s dedication to offering platforms for them to display their skills. This intense competition not only improves their skills but also plays a role in their general personal growth and achievement.
